Cheese garlic grilled sandwich 
1. add spices to butter (1 teaspoon red chilly powder,pinch of garam masala, pinch of
 turmeric) 
2. apply the butter which we have created on the bread slices 
3. add grated cheese on the bread slice 
4. add oregano and chilly flakes on the cheese 
5. add chhopped garlic 
6. apply the second slice of bread
7. grill the sandwich with the help of (tava,griller,microwave-oven)
